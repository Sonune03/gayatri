About Me

Hi, I’m Gayatri Sonune, an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering student at MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune, with a strong passion for technology, automation, and artificial intelligence. My expertise includes sensor integration, Blynk setup, Python programming, MATLAB & SIMULINK, and PLC designing.

I have gained hands-on experience through various projects, including an AI-powered virtual fashion stylist, IoT-based environmental monitoring using ESP8266, and underwater ROV development. Additionally, my internship at Rukmini Coatings Pvt. Ltd. involved designing PLC systems for factory automation, enhancing my practical knowledge in industrial automation.

With certifications in C programming, MATLAB Simulink, and cybersecurity, I am always eager to learn new technologies and apply them effectively in real-world applications. My goal is to contribute to innovative projects that bridge electronics, automation, and AI, making technology more efficient and accessible.
